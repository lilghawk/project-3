My Music Taste

Introduction

Arabic Music is a big part of my life. I appreciate artists who create meaningful songs. My favorite singers include Mohammed Abdu, Mehad Hamad, and Angham, all of whom have a deep impact on Arabic music.

Mohammed Abdu

Mohammed Abdu, known as the "Artist of the Arabs," is a legendary Saudi singer famous for his classical Arabic music and poetic lyrics.

Mehad Hamad

Mehad Hamad is an Emirati singer known for his deep voice and traditional Gulf music style. His songs often reflect heritage and nostalgia.

Angham

Angham is an Egyptian singer with a powerful voice and a unique ability to convey deep emotions through her music.
